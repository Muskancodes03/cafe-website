Our Story

Brewed with Passion,
Served with Soul

Noir & Gold began as a single roasting drum in the back of a Lahore side-street shop. Today it's a destination for people who care about where their coffee comes from and how it's made. We source directly from smallholder farms, roast in small batches every week, and train every barista on our floor to treat each cup as a craft, not a commodity.

Step inside and you'll find warm brass fixtures, low light, the hiss of the espresso machine, and a menu built around beans that changed the way we think about flavour.
